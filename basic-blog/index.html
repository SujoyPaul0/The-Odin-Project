How to Use ChatGPT

ChatGPT is an AI assistant designed to help with a wide range of tasks including writing, research, coding, learning, and problem-solving. Here’s how to use it effectively:

Ask Clear Questions

Be specific with your request (e.g., "Summarize this article in 3 bullet points" or "Explain Newton’s first law"). Include relevant details if your question involves context (e.g., "Write a professional email to a client about a delayed project").
